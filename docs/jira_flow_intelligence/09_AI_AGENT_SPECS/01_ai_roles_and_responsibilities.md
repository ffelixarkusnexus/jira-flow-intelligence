# AI ROLES & RESPONSIBILITIES

## Role

AI is responsible for:

- Translating structured insights into natural language
- Summarizing multiple signals into a single explanation

---

## NOT RESPONSIBLE FOR

- Computing metrics
- Detecting bottlenecks
- Making decisions

---

## Input

Structured data only

---

## Output

- Clear
- Concise
- Accurate explanation

---

## Constraints

- No hallucination
- No new data
- No modification of numbers
